Why Practice Tai Chi? 為什麼練太極拳

Wu Style Tai Chi is a complete system of health, meditation, and self-defense for every stage of life.

Stress Reduction

Gentle movements and focused breathing calm the mind and body.

Improved Balance

Regular practice improves coordination and helps prevent falls.

Physical Fitness

Low-impact exercise for all ages — strength, flexibility, and endurance.

Emotional Wellbeing

Reduces anxiety and depression; promotes a positive mood.

Chronic Disease Management

Assists with COPD, fibromyalgia, and many other conditions.

Cognitive Function

Enhances focus, memory, and mental clarity through mindful movement.

Our Heritage 我們的傳承

Over 30 Years Serving Metro Detroit

Founded in Shanghai in 1935 by Grandmaster Wu Chien-ch'uan, the Wu Style lineage now reaches Ferndale, Michigan. For over three decades our Academy has taught seniors, hospital patients, teenagers, and everyday people across 24 satellite locations and our main school.

"Softness overcomes hardness, and hardness reinforces softness." — Grandmaster Wu Kung-tsao
